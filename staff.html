A Message From Our Founder

At Mavuno Trade, we're revolutionizing agricultural exports from Tanzania. Since 2024, we've built bridges between local farmers and international markets while maintaining the highest standards of quality and ethical business practices.

Our farmer-first approach connects over 100 smallholder farmers across Tanzania with premium global markets they couldn't access alone. This creates traceability, quality control, and real economic impact in rural communities.

Our team combines agricultural expertise with international trade experience. From soil specialists to logistics experts, every member shares our commitment to excellence and sustainability.

Josiah Mitekaro Founder & Chief Executive Officer
