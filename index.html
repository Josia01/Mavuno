Welcome to Twing Memorial

Founded in 1995, Twing Memorial School has been providing exceptional education to our community for over 25 years. Our dual-campus approach ensures specialized learning environments for each age group.

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Twing Memorial School was established with the vision of creating a comprehensive educational institution that would serve students from kindergarten through high school graduation. Our unique twin-campus model allows us to provide age-appropriate facilities and programs while maintaining a cohesive school community.

We pride ourselves on our student-centered approach, with small class sizes that allow for personalized attention and differentiated instruction to meet each student's needs.
