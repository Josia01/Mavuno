About Us

Mavuno Trade Ltd, headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, is a trusted name in the agricultural export industry. With deep roots in local farming communities and strong logistics capabilities, we ensure that premium Tanzanian produce reaches markets across East Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and Europe.

We are committed to sustainability, transparency, and long-term partnerships that uplift local farmers and deliver value to our global clients. Our team combines agricultural expertise with international trade knowledge to bridge the gap between Tanzanian farms and world markets.

By maintaining strict quality control measures and ethical sourcing practices, we guarantee that our products meet both international standards and our clients' specific requirements.
