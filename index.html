About Mavuno Trade

Mavuno Trade Ltd exports premium Tanzanian agricultural produce to global markets. Based in Dar es Salaam, we connect East African farms to the world.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and fair trade practices ensures that we deliver the finest products while supporting local farming communities.

With a focus on transparency and long-term partnerships, we bridge the gap between Tanzanian farmers and international markets, providing reliable supply chain solutions.

Our Products

Premium Coffee

High-quality Arabica and Robusta coffee beans sourced from the rich volcanic soils of Tanzania.

Cashew Nuts

Organically grown cashew nuts, carefully processed to maintain their natural flavor and nutrients.

Organic Spices

A variety of aromatic spices including cloves, cinnamon, and black pepper, grown without pesticides.
