Our Company

Founded in 2024, Mavuno Trade Ltd has grown from a small agricultural trading company to one of Tanzania's leading exporters of premium agricultural products. Headquartered in Dar es Salaam, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality Tanzanian agricultural commodities to markets across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and Europe.

Our name "Mavuno" means "harvest" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to helping our partners reap the benefits of Tanzania's rich agricultural potential. We work directly with over 500 smallholder farmers and cooperatives across Tanzania, ensuring fair prices and sustainable farming practices.

With state-of-the-art processing facilities and a team of agricultural experts, we maintain strict quality control at every stage of the supply chain. Our logistics network ensures timely delivery to our international clients while maintaining product quality and freshness.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our Vision

Our Journey

Key milestones in Mavuno Trade Ltd's growth and development

2024

Company Founded

Mavuno Trade Ltd was established in Dar es Salaam with a focus on domestic maize and rice trading.

2024

First Export Contract

Secured our first international client, exporting 50 tons of Tanzanian rice to Kenya.

2025

Processing Facility Opened

Established our first grain processing and packaging facility in Morogoro.

2025

EU Certification

Received European Union certification for our processing facilities, opening new markets.

2025

Expanded Product Line

Added timber and specialty crops to our export portfolio, diversifying our offerings.
